Commission Structure

5 formation centers and vocation promotion programs

Overview

The Commission on Formation & Vocation guides the spiritual, intellectual, and pastoral development of Brothers throughout their religious journey. Four formation houses serve different stages of religious life, supported by Montfort Spirituality Center and vocation promotion outreach.

St. Louis Mary Juvenate

ยุวลัยเซนต์หลุยส์แมรี่ — Primary formation center for young postulants discovering religious life and charism of the congregation.

St. Gabriel Scholasticate

บ้านนักศึกษาคณะเซนต์คาเบรียล — Intensive theological and spiritual preparation for professed Brothers preparing for ordination and apostolic mission.

Montfort Novitiate

นวกสถาน เดอ มงฟอร์ต — Sacred initiation year for new novices entering the vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience in religious community.

Montfort Studies Center

ศูนย์มงฟอร์ตศึกษา — Advanced study of Montfort spirituality, theology, and legacy. Continuing education for all Brothers.

Vocation Promotion

งานส่งเสริมกระแสเรียก — Outreach to youth, school visits, discernment programs, and invitation to religious life in the congregation.
