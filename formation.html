Vocation Development & Religious Formation

งานพัฒนากระแสเรียกและการฝึกอบรมนักบวช

The Commission on Formation & Vocation guides the spiritual, intellectual, and pastoral development of Brothers throughout their religious journey — from the first stirring of vocation, through the Juvenate, Scholasticate, and Novitiate, to ongoing Montfort Studies for perpetually professed Brothers.

This Commission is supported by the Saint Gabriel's Foundation (Thailand) which provides administrative and financial backing — while the spiritual and apostolic life of formation belongs to the Province of Thailand as a religious community.

Four Formation Houses

บ้านอบรม 4 หลัง ของแขวงประเทศไทย

St. Louis Marie Juvenate

ยุวลัยเซนต์หลุยส์แมรี่

The first formation house, where young men discerning religious life begin formal preparation under the guidance of senior Brothers.

St. Gabriel Scholasticate

บ้านนักศึกษาเซนต์คาเบรียล

Scholastics deepen their studies in theology, education, and Montfortian spirituality while preparing for first vows.

Montfort Novitiate

บ้านนวกรรมมงฟอร์ต

The novitiate year of intensive prayer, community life, and discernment before professing first vows as a Brother of Saint Gabriel.

Montfort Studies Center

สำนักงานมงฟอร์ตศึกษา

Ongoing formation for perpetually professed Brothers — deepening Montfortian spirituality, mission renewal, and lifelong learning.

Vocation Promotion

งานส่งเสริมกระแสเรียก

Outreach to schools and Catholic communities across Thailand to invite young men to consider the consecrated life as Brothers of Saint Gabriel. Vocation promoters travel between schools, retreats, and parishes — sharing the Montfortian charism through testimony, accompaniment, and discernment retreats.

Interested in learning more? Contact the Vocation Promotion office through any Brother of the Province, or via the Saint Gabriel's Foundation.

About this page: Religious formation is the core vocation of the Province of Thailand — this is why Formation & Vocation lives on Montfortian Network rather than on the Foundation site. The Foundation supports formation administratively; the religious life itself is shaped by the Province.
